United States of Design is the investment studio of Frank Müller-Pierstorff — founder, creative strategist, and former global creative director at BCG. I back early-stage founders who build with purpose, challenge dominant logic, and cultivate new mental models for how we live, relate, and progress. If that sounds like you, reach out.

I’m drawn to ideas that carry meaning and the courage to shift systems — not just scale them. From rethinking communication, health, money, or food, to creating new forms of value and connection.

Beyond capital, I help shape purpose, brand, culture, and creativity — whatever it takes to make the right idea land, grow, and resonate. I support founders in pivotal moments — by asking the right questions and helping make the right calls.
